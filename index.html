About

Creative Specialist. AI Native. Production Trained.

I'm a multi-disciplinary creative with a Bachelor of Arts in CGI and Animation, trained in classical production at studios like Chocolate Tribe and Wicked Pixels — where I contributed to the Netflix series Queen Sono, a full CGI Lexus advert, and national ad campaigns.

Today, I combine that production foundation with fluency across a full suite of generative AI tools — both imagery and video — to deliver assets that are not just generated, but production-ready. Every output goes through professional Photoshop post-production or DaVinci Resolve finishing before it reaches a client.

As a Top-Rated freelancer on Upwork with a 100% Job Success Score, I bring the same standard to every project: precise prompt engineering, meticulous documentation, and work that clients actually want to use.
